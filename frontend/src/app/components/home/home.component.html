AI Pipeline

How UrbanPulse Works

Three specialized AI agents collaborate behind the scenes to ensure every citizen report is handled with speed, accuracy, and fairness.

01

Citizen Reports

Any resident submits an incident report with location, description, and optional photo via the web form.

02

Classifier Agent

Analyses the text, location context, and historical data to assign a category and a 1–5 priority score.

03

Planner Agent

Considers department workloads and active incidents in the district to route the task optimally.

04

Monitor Agent

Polls open incidents every 15 minutes, detects SLA breaches, and escalates or closes cases automatically.
